FAQ

Got Questions?

Clear answers to the things parents most often ask before joining Little Wise Kids.

What age range do you care for?

We care for children from 6 months to 5 years old, providing a safe, nurturing and engaging early years environment tailored to each stage of development.

What languages are part of the curriculum?

Portuguese and Spanish are introduced through songs, stories, play and everyday routines, so children experience language in a natural and enjoyable way.

What are your opening hours?

We are open Monday to Friday from 7:00 to 18:00. We are open through school holidays and closed on bank holidays.

Where are you located?

Little Wise Kids Childminding is based in a home setting just behind Cabot Circus car park, with easy access to town and the M32.

What is your childminding approach?

Our approach combines traditional Early Years learning with modern languages and cultures. We focus on play, care, confidence, curiosity and strong partnerships with families.
